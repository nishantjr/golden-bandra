The 'O' community of our parish is located at Benmar and Rajaram Wadi. There are 83 families residing here. The patron of this community is St. Maria Goretti.


The community Rosary was recited at 8 different locations on Tuesdays and Fridays during the month of October.

We conducted a Prayer Service for the Rite of Acceptance Of Confirmation for 5 candidates in our community.

For Christmas, we distributed gifts (dolls for the girls and a bat and ball for the boys) as well as packets of biscuits and Peppys to 30 poor children of construction workers.
