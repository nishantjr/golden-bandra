Finance Committee

This began as the 'Ways and Means Committe' founded by Fr Henry Remedios in June 1950. The responsibilities of this committee are to allocate and oversee the various expenditures for routine and special projects. The recent renovation and major repairs conducted on the Bosco Hall building is an example of the work sanctioned by the Committee. The Finance committee consists of Joe Pereira, Ms.Charmaine Barretto, Ernest Fernandes and Henry D\'Souza.Of course the Head as always is the Parish Priest (Rev. Fr Vernon Aguiar).
